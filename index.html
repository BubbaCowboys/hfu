About Us

Student led Organization

We have three main goals: giving the homeless a better understanding of current health, showing them they have the power to achieve a healthy lifestyle, and informing them of primary care providers around them. In pursuit of goal three, we launched our National Primary Care Resources Project in Summer 2020. Over 80 volunteers from around the country worked to create comprehensive Resource Guides to low-cost healthcare resources in their communities and distribute them to places such as homeless shelters and soup kitchens that are frequented by homeless individuals.
